Trading Rules

RULES ACTIVE
Apne trading rules ek baar set karo — har naye trade ka Discipline Score automatically inhi rules ke against check hoga.
1. Maximum Trades Per Day
Agar tum ek din me isse zyada trades loge, to Discipline Score kam ho jayega.
2. Minimum Risk : Reward
Agar kisi trade ka RR isse kam hai, to Discipline Score kam ho jayega.
3. Maximum Risk Per Trade
Agar koi trade isse zyada % risk karta hai, to Discipline Score kam ho jayega.
4. Maximum Consecutive Losses Per Day
Set the maximum number of consecutive losing trades you allow yourself in a single trading day.
5. Stop Trading After Winning Trades
Set the maximum number of winning trades after which you should stop trading for the day.
